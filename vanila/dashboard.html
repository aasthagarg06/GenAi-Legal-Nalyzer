Risk Flags

High Security Deposit

The security deposit of $3,000 (2x monthly rent) is higher than the state maximum of 1.5x monthly rent.

Action: Negotiate to reduce the deposit to $2,250 or less.

Strict Pet Policy

No pets allowed without written consent, with a non-refundable pet fee of $500.

Action: If you have pets, negotiate terms before signing.

Early Termination Clause

Breaking the lease early requires 60 days notice and 2 months rent penalty.

Action: Consider if you might need to move before lease ends.
